My Life on Kaizen (Part 4)

Aug 6, 2018

This is a point that is often discussed so I wanted to bring it up too:

How To Catch Up

We’ve all been there. 1 day behind. 1 week behind. Even a whole semester behind with the exam in two days. How do you catch up. Well it does depend on how far behind you really are. There is a different level of commitment and prioritisation that is required for each specific condition. Depending on the severity of the gap, we need to adjust this to fit.

The Miracle of Imperfection

I used to think that imperfection was a flaw. That any work at I did had to be done exactly by the time it was due in satisfactory condition. This, it turns out, is anything but the case. Kaizen follows exactly this principle. It distinguishes the changes from their outcome. It shows us that it is not necessarily terrible that we cannot be perfect, we must mind it and fix our flaws using incremental changes.
