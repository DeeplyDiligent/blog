My Life on Kaizen (Part 2)

Aug 3, 2018

Trying a Kaizen strategy in my application strategy for vacation and internship positions has got me thinking, how else can I use it to enhance my life, so that I am more energized, connected and can achieve more every day. Even just this mindset, no matter how small, can create the environment that is required to insinuate change.

The importance of holding yourself accountable

During the beginning of this semester, I listed out the activities which I would have to complete each and every day with one key request. The request was:

Write down exactly why you skipped this activity this week, including stuff which you could have done better in the future to make sure this doesnt happen again.

By doing this, I got my brain into the habit of thinking about time as a precious entity and utilising it to the best of my abilities.

Next, I will take a look at what else I have done to ensure activities are completed, including setting due dates and tailoring activities to fit my other scheduled classes and work commitments.
