Let's Talk: A Blog About Life Aspirations and Success

Jul 13, 2018

At the beginning there was nothing. Life didn’t even exist. We were created through years of evolution, centuries of change, millenia of catastrophies and era’s of knowledge and turmoil. Yet one of the most mind boggling questions that we always ask is…

Why Do I Exist?

Look i’ll be honest I have no clue. Noone has any clue. Even when youre in heaven. Even when you’re farting fairy dust in your fancy Ferrari and three story mansion. You’ll still have no clue what you’re doing.

Why Try?

I’m glad you asked. You see, many people make up their own mind about this after reading that theres no magic point to life. They realise that the dream house, the cushy job, the fancy wife are not all they are made up to be. So they quit. These are the so called “mid life crisis” points of our lives. We don’t know what we worked so hard to build. We suffer from the “hedonic tredmill” of always jumping on the next supposed high. Get this, eat that, try this, experience that. Its like a never ending cycle of misery.

How can we get a purpose?

We will be answering this question, along with how to set proper goals in tommorrow’s post. Stay tuned!
