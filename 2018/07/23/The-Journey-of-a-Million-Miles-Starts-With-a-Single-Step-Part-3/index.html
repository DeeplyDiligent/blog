Spaceship Analogy

Ever seen a spaceship launch? I know that the glamour and amazement of watching the controlled explosion of metric tonnes of fuel leaves me excited and bewildered.

Let me ask you this though: How much time do you reckon a spaceship bound for the mood spends on course? 90%? 80%? Nope nope. Throughout the whole process of taking off, going into orbit, passing the point where the moon’s gravity overtakes the earth and landing on the moon, the spaceship is on couse only 10% of time time. That’s right!
