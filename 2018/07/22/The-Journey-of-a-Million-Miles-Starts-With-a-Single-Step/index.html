The Journey of a Million Miles Starts With a Single Step

Jul 22, 2018

Lately I have been getting incredibly into researching a unique and valuable priciple known as Kaizen. Over the next few blogposts, I wish to take you through what Kaizen is, and how to apply it in your own life.

A Brief Intro to Most People’s life

Alright here we are, average. We go through 12 years of schooling, learning the bare minimum to get by. We hate learning, but know that we have to do it to get into a field that we can tolerate for the next 40 years. We confuse education and schooling, thinking that they are one and the same. We go get a bachaelors degree in a non-STEM field, hoping that the degree will be enough for us to get into a job. We try to focus and study but end up drunk at our best mate’s sister’s 21st Birthday Party. We mostly enjoy college, taking a bit of time off during exam time to cram some last minute revision, resenting every bit of it. During exams, we struggle to die down our nerves and due to a lack of proper exam technique, get mediocre results. Getting into jobs seems very difficult and so we settle. We settle for small companies paying mediocre wages. And we settle for a lackluster life of fullfilling family, management and social expectations.

Now I completely understand everyone who has a medical and other circumstance and I am in no way suggesting everyone is like this. However, in my life experience, this is pretty much stock standard Average. Ill be looking into improvements that we could make on this in tomorrow’s post!
