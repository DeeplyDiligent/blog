The Journey of a Million Miles Starts With a Single Step (Part 4)

Jul 24, 2018

Here is a way to measure yourself against Performance Metrics: A simple timetable.

Out of simple things, big things grow

I once had a lemon plant. It didn’t grow very much in the Australian winter. Eventually it’s leaves started falling off and it was barely surviving.

That summer, I was forced to do a little bit of research on plantation. I learnt how to care for lemon trees in Australia and how they can grow up with just a little care, effort, and most importantly…fertiliser!

This is why Kaizen is so important. Keeping a very simple timetable will allow you to measure yourself against something quantifiable. It was also very easy to make on paper, so it would be hard to skip something that quick.

Why it’s important

Imagine you’re like me, trying to grow a lemon tree. Unless you measured the effect of each thing you tried: watering it, using different types of fertiliser, how would you know which one is working and which is not. It’s crucial to have a metric, since without it, the whole endeavour can go awfully awry.
